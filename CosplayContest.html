Contest Rules

(1) You must sign up to the cosplay contest to be in the contest all age ranges are welcome to join

(2) All cosplays must be appropriate as this ia a all ages event

(3) You must say what your cosplay is in the query before the event

(4) Cosplay must be made by the person and cant be completly both online

(5) cosplays can have props but props cant be made of wood or metal

How to sign up

if you would like to sign up you can by doing these simple steps

(1) go to our contact page on this website

(2) fill in your information in all the fields

(3) and in the query section specify its for the cosplay contest and tell us the cosplay you made and how you made it
